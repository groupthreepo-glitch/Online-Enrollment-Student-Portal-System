Gardner College Cainta Online Enrollment and Student Portal System

Effective Date: August 25, 2025
Last Updated: August 19, 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using the Gardner College Cainta Online Enrollment and Student Portal System ("the System"), you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use the System.

2. System Description and Purpose

The Gardner College Cainta Online Enrollment and Student Portal System is designed to:

  • Facilitate online enrollment for prospective and current students
  • Provide access to academic records, grades, and schedules
  • Enable communication between students, faculty, and administration
  • Manage course registration and academic planning
  • Process payments for tuition and fees

3. User Eligibility and Account Registration

3.1 Eligibility

  • Students: Current and prospective students of Gardner College Cainta
  • Faculty: Authorized teaching staff and academic personnel
  • Administrator: Administrative personnel with approved access
  • Registrar: Authorized Handling Student Information

3.2 Account Registration

  • Users must provide accurate, complete, and current information
  • Each user is responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials
  • Users must immediately notify the college of any unauthorized use of their account
  • False information provided during registration may result in account termination

4. User Responsibilities and Conduct

4.1 Acceptable Use

Users agree to:

  • Use the System only for legitimate educational and administrative purposes
  • Respect the privacy and rights of other users
  • Comply with all applicable laws and college policies
  • Maintain the security of their account credentials

4.2 Prohibited Activities

Users shall not:

  • Share login credentials with unauthorized individuals
  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to restricted areas
  • Upload malicious software or harmful content
  • Use the System for commercial purposes without authorization
  • Interfere with the System's operation or security features
  • Engage in harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior

5. Privacy and Data Protection

5.1 Information Collection

The System collects and processes:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, contact details)
  • Academic records and educational history
  • Financial information for payment processing
  • System usage data and logs

6. Academic Information and Records

Users are responsible for verifying the accuracy of their academic information. Any discrepancies should be reported immediately to the Registrar's Office.

7. Financial Responsibilities

All financial transactions through the System are subject to verification. Payment confirmations do not guarantee enrollment until all requirements are met.

8. System Availability and Maintenance

The college strives to maintain system availability but cannot guarantee uninterrupted service. Scheduled maintenance will be announced in advance when possible.
